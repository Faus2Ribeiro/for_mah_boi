WAKU WAKU!

Branding

I had a project that I had to create a new brand. My idea was to create a platform for portuguese viewers of japanese animation.

Process

I decided to go with a flashy yellow color over the exclamation mark to help it stand out more from the rest.

Meaning

WAKU WAKU is a japanese onomatopoeia that expresses excitement. I decided to use it since most anime viewers tend to feel this excitement when a new episode of their favourite series is released.
